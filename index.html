About us

Here at Stinóra we believe in creating the most robust solutions to your hectic schedules

Our DNA Stinora was conceived in response to the growing need for a comprehensive project tracker that goes beyond the conventional. Developed with the understanding that effective project management is pivotal in both academic and professional realms, Stenora stands as a solution tailored to empower users in navigating the complexities of tasks, deadlines, and collaborative endeavors. Its primary objective is to provide a user friendly website for students and professionals alike to make managing projects as easy as possible giving alerts on unfinished projects , monitor deadlines and manage time to be spent making each projects.
